Learning: Our Courses

Courses offered at our Main Campus and City Campus

Our main campus is at Coffee Plaza, Nairobi CBD, Kenya. The City Campus is at 2nd Floor, Kenda Hse, Nairobi.


Diploma Courses (C- and above, 2yrs:)

  • Secretarial/Front office (Computerized secretarial).
  • Diploma in Community Development and Social Work.
  • Diploma in Business Management.
  • Diploma in Human Resource Management.
  • Diploma in Project Management.
  • Diploma in Information Communication Technology.
  • Diploma in Electrical Engineering.
  • Diploma in Catering and Accommodation.


Certificate Courses (D plain and above, 1yr):

  • Certificate in Community Development and Social Work.
  • Certificate in Business Management.
  • Certificate in Project Management.
  • Certificate in Information Communication Technology.
  • Certificate in E|ectrical Engineering.
  • Certificate in Catering and Accommodation.


Artisan Courses (D plain and below):

  • Food and Beverage.
  • Electrical Installation.
  • Store Keeping.


KASNEB Programmes:

  • Accounting Technician Diploma (ADT); C- and above
  • Certified Public Accountant (CPA); C+ and above, C+ in Eng & Math.



Courses offered at Mtito Andei Campus.

Our Mtito Andei campus is at Machinery Market, Mtito Andei.


Diploma Courses (C- and above, 2yrs):

  • Diploma in Community Development and Social Work.
  • Diploma in Early Childhood Development (ECDE).
  • Diploma in Business Management.
  • Diploma in Primary Teacher Education (P1).
  • Diploma in Teacher Education (DTE).


Certificate Courses (D plain and above, 1yr):

  • Certificate in Community Development and Social Work.
  • Certificate in Business Management.
  • Diploma in Early Childhood Development (ECDE).
  • Certificate in Agriculture/Agribusiness.


Artisan Courses (D plain and below):

  • Proficiency (ECDE).
