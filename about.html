About us

Is a non profit-making Christian based institution. Since its inception it has facilitated several community developments to disadvantaged students who are not able to access tertiary education due to financial constraints.

Milestones

Milele Foundation was formed with a vision to create a self-reliant community socially, economically and politically; giving our mentees the opportunity to rediscover their taalents and to pursue their life goals without fear.

Milele Institute of Development and Technology was born of Milele Foundation, and so was Milele Teachers Training College.

  • Our faculties organise teaching and research into individual subjects.
  • Several years of people, and achievements that continue to transform.
  • The foundation's core values are as follows: freedom of thought,innovation and industry.
